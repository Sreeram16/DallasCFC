Welcome to TGCF Church

We are a non demoninational independent church. We boldly approach the throne of Grace to press on to perfection in our personal and family life, putting our past behind. We also desire to be perfected in unity as a body of believers, in accordance with our Lord's prayer in John 17:23.

  • Philippians 3:12 Not that I have already obtained it or have already become perfect, but I press on so that I may lay hold of that for which also I was laid hold of by Christ

  • Hebrews 4:16 Therefore let us draw near with confidence to the throne of grace, so that we may receive mercy and find grace to help in time of need.

  • John 17:23 I in them and You in Me, that they may be perfected in unity,...
